MUNNAR :

Munnar's cool climate and serene beauty provide a perfect escape from the heat and bustle of the cities. The area's vast tea plantations, scenic viewpoints, and unique flora and fauna make it a must-visit destination in Kerala. Munnar is not only a haven for relaxation but also offers trekking, boating, and wildlife spotting, catering to diverse interests and making it one of Kerala's most popular hill stations.
